Structure

First semester

October 1:
Opening ceremony of the academic year
October 1 - December 23:
12 weeks - teaching activity
December 24 - January 6:
2 weeks: winter break
January 7 - January 20:
2 weeks - teaching activity
January 21 - February 3:
2 weeks - evaluation
February 4 - 17:
2 weeks - holiday
within which 1 week - re-examination session
February 11 - February 24:
session for final graduation examination

Second semester (for non-final years)

February 18 - June 2:
14 weeks - teaching activity
1 week off during the Easter holidays (April 29 - May 5)
June 3 - June 16:
2 weeks - evaluation
June 17 - July 7:
1 week - re-examination session
The academic records for 2018-2019 will be finalized on July 7.
July 8 - September 30:
Summer break

Second semester (for final years)

February 18 - June 2:
14 weeks - teaching activity
1 week off during the Easter holidays (April 29 - May 5)
June 3 - June 16:
2 weeks - evaluation
June 17 - June 30:
2 weeks: finalization of the degree bachelor/master paper
During this period, a 1-week re-examination session will be organized.
Registration for the final graduation exam will be carried out during the last week of this interval.
July 1 - July 7:
Final graduation exam
